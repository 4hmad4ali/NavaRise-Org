Privacy Policy

This document explains how NavaRise collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: 2026-01-30

1) What information do we collect?

We only collect information that is necessary for communication, registration, or improving our services. This may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address and phone number
  • Your message title and content (via the contact form)
  • Program registration details (if you register through Google Forms or similar tools)

2) How do we collect information?

We typically receive your information through website forms (such as Contact or Donation Pledge), or through third-party forms/services (such as Google Forms or Formspree).

3) How do we use your information?

  • To respond to your messages and requests
  • To coordinate registrations and run classes and webinars
  • To send relevant updates (only when needed and related to your request)
  • To improve program quality and user experience

4) Do we share your information with others?

NavaRise does not sell your personal information and does not share it with third parties for advertising purposes. However, in some cases your data may be shared in a limited way with services we use, such as:

  • Formspree to receive contact/donation form messages
  • Google Forms for program registrations (when used)
  • Email services used to receive and respond to requests

These services have their own privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing them as well.

5) Cookies and analytics

At this time, NavaRise does not use advertising cookies. If analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) are added in the future, this page will be updated.

6) Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information; however, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Please avoid sending sensitive information (such as passwords) through website forms.

7) Data retention

We keep information only as long as necessary to respond, follow up on requests, or support educational/operational needs. When it is no longer needed, the data may be deleted or archived.

8) Your rights

You may request:

  • Correction of your information
  • A copy of the information we hold about you
  • Deletion of your information (when feasible and subject to operational/legal requirements)

9) Children’s privacy

Because NavaRise works with children and youth, protecting their privacy is extremely important to us. Any information about children is collected only within the educational context and using a minimal approach, and we avoid making such information public.

10) External links

Our site may contain links to other websites or services. NavaRise is not responsible for their privacy practices.

11) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated date will always appear at the top of this page.
